Project Overview

In your group, use what you learned from the 4 modules (cybersecurity, digital narratives, economic & social crises, cultural heritage & AI) to design one integrated solution for one concrete problem (preferably in Jerusalem / Palestine / Morocco).

Slide Structure Suggestions

1

2

The problem (human story)

  • Who is affected? Where? What is happening?

  • Link it to at least two of the modules (e.g. security + economy, narratives + heritage, etc.)
5-7

Solution plan

  • What is your idea?
  • Who are the users?

  • Sketch or describe: a portal, dashboard, campaign, assistant, app, or video concept
10

Next step / vision (optional)

  • If you had more time and resources, what would the full solution look like?
